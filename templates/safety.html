Safety Tips for Photo Buddy Users

  • Meet in public places for any photo exchange or collaboration.
  • Let someone know where you're going and who you’re meeting.
  • Trust your instincts—if something feels off, end the interaction.
  • Avoid sharing personal information (phone number, address, etc.) in chats.
  • Report suspicious behavior using our in-app reporting tools or by contacting support.
  • Do not exchange money or valuables with new contacts unless you feel safe and there’s a clear agreement.
  • Respect boundaries; everyone deserves a safe and positive experience.
  • Review profiles before agreeing to meet and communicate your expectations clearly.
